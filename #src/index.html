History

A City literally built on Rock n’ Roll. Music Festival is the true Rock experience you can’t miss. In its 4th year, it is THE top venue for people of all ages to come together for one common purpose: to congregate with other rock fans from across the world, in a place where rock music still matters.

Location
Music Festival is set on 320 acres of picturesque land in Central Rockwille, and is home to nearly 7,000 campsites all within easy walking distance of the concert grounds.

Experience the Amphitheater Venue for yourself, and find out why Rock Fest Fans keep coming back every year to the Largest Rock & Camping Festival in the US.
